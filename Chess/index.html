Chess Rules

Piece Movement
♔ King One square in any direction
♕ Queen Any number of squares in any direction
♖ Rook Any number of squares horizontally or vertically
♗ Bishop Any number of squares diagonally
♘ Knight "L" shape: Two squares in one direction, then one sideways
♙ Pawn Moves forward one, captures diagonally, can move two on first move

Special Moves

  • Castling: King moves two squares towards a rook; rook jumps over.
  • En Passant: A pawn can capture an adjacent pawn that moved two squares.
  • Promotion: Pawns reaching the last rank promote to a queen, rook, bishop, or knight.

Win Conditions

  • Checkmate: The king is trapped with no legal moves.
  • Stalemate: A player has no legal moves, but the king is not in check (draw).
  • Draw: Insufficient material, threefold repetition, or 50-move rule.
